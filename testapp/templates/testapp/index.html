Our Mission

As the world changes, so must the education system. Yet, this should not be taken as a threat, rather, as an opportunity to do better.

One challenge faced by educators is the fact that students all learn at their own paces. At times this means that students miss what their teachers are saying. Sometimes, this is corrected by exttra classes or tuition. However, there are not enough teachers to hold extra classes whenever a student has questions.

Fortunately, they do not need to. By creating a space online for discussion, both current and senior students can contribute to answer questions about the specific topic. This lightens the load on teachers and allows them to easily test the understanding of their students. Additionally, senior students can revise the material and extend their understanding

This platform also allows teachers and students to plan out extra classes, which remain important as some questions are better explained in a physical setting. Teachers and any student with deep knowledge of the subject material can hold these classes.

Features and Benefits

Grouped by Topic

Allows students to easily find questions to revise or answer.

Vote on class times

Students can agree on the best time for extra classes so everyone who wants to can join

Better learning outcomes

Studies have shown that learning by teaching is extremely effective. Additionally, students get reputation points for answering questions and teaching classes. These points are evidence of the student's contributions and can be helpful in university applications as they show both knowledge and generosity.
